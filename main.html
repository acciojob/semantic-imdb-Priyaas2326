Quotes

Kantara movie is designed based on the culture of coastal karnataka (Tulunadu), which is amazingly depicted with world class acting of all crews and mainly by Rishabh shetty.

The story of Kantara (sacred forest) sets in south-west coastal region of Karnataka. It encompasses three layers of timelines; the royal history of late 40s of nineteenth century, late 70s of twentieth century and late 90s of the same which forms the major part of the film story. A restless king promises his lands to the local tribals in exchange of a stone (Panjurli, a deity of Keradi Village) wherein he finds solace and peace of mind.

Summary

Set in a fictional village of Dakshina Kannada, Kantara brings alive the traditional culture of Kambla and Bhootha Kola. It is believed that Demigods are the guardians and their energies encircle the village.In the story, there is a ripple when a battle of ego swirls among the traditions and culture of the land. In a conflict between humanity and nature, Shiva is in rebellion and working against the latter. He engages in intense conflicts leading up to a war between the villagers and evil forces. Can Shiva reinstate peace and harmony?
